About Me

Hello all, my name is Johnpaul Ciccosanti, I am a Biomedical Engineering student at Arizona State University, I was also a member of the Barrett Honors College for some of my time here. In regard to education, I have a strong passion for Biology and am primarily interested in how far we can take the human body from the standpoint of genetic manipulation. Moreover, my passions have diversified over the years and while I still hold that same strong passion, I now understand how the various fields intertwine an am looking to dwelve into the field of Mechanical Engineering, particularely, fluid dynamics and or nanorobotics. I am relatively open and am curious to see where my educational pursuits will take me.

Given that I am an open person, many of my hobbies and passions are varied and not necessarily correlated in any standard way. I will try pretty much anything at least once given that open-minded mindset. Currently, my main hobbies consist of working on and modifying performance vehicles, bodybuilding and nutrition, as well as some computer gaming. However, this is a much simplified list and there are many others things that I could passionately talk about all day.

The primary goal of this website is to essentially serve as a portfolio of my life and activities. While making such a website does seem rather conceited, I think that it is important considering that with such a diversified set of skills and interests, it is hard to concisely answer simple questions such as "what do you like to do" or "what type of person are you". I am hoping that this website will primarily be used for employment purposes, or perhaps as a resume of some sort, or used to find a business partner. However, in the future, if I am able to be successful in any of the projects or businesses that I am planning on creating, I think that the website could easily be modified to contain things like a store or products, perhaps I consider it the beginning of something great.
